About EUGLUG

EUGLUG is the Eugene Unix and GNU/Linux User Group, an informal group of enthusiasts of Unix and Unix-like operating systems, including (but not limited to) open source variants such as Linux.

We welcome anyone interested,
regardless of experience level.

IRC Channel

There is an #euglug IRC channel on Freenode that is loosely related to the group. If you are having difficulties with Linux or Unix-related issues; it's not a bad place to ask questions. Be warned that it is often filled with silliness and off-topic conversations.

Reddit

In lieu of a mailing list, we've got our own subreddit at /r/EUGLUG. In the usual style of the site (and the group), anything linuxy goes.

Monthly Presentations

Once a month we have a presentation on a technical topic. These are generally held on the first Tuesday of the month, at 6 PM. For details, see the Meetup page.

Weekly Meetings

We meet on Thursday evenings at 7 PM. These are highly informal, laid-back affairs, and a good place to come with Linux-related questions and pleas for help.

To find out the current meeting location, send email to info@euglug.org.
